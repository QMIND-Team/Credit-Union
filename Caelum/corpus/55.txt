Alberta Central represents all credit unions across the province - more than 611,000 system members served by almost 200 branches.

Our purpose
To champion change in the credit union system and bring value and expertise to credit unions.

 

Our core values 
Our values are guiding principles that communicate who we are, what we do, how we do it and why we do it. Our values don’t drive our organization, but rather, drive the people in our organization.

Our core values are:

Empowerment
Responsibility
Cooperative
Customer Service
Our commitment to these values brings success to our employees, our organization and the Alberta credit union system.

Alberta credit unions are anchored in the communities we serve. We believe in providing resources that help communities achieve success.
