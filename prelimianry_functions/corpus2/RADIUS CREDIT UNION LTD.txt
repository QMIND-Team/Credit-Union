  About Us

Welcome to Radius Credit Union Ltd. We are a full service financial
institution serving several communities in Southern Saskatchewan. We have
branches in Avonlea, Ceylon, Ogema, Pangman, Radville, Torquay and Tribune. We
have been serving the area for over 60 years!

At Radius Credit Union we are continually working to update our knowledge, to
ensure you, our members, receive the best possible service and most up to date
products.We continuously update our technology so that you have access to
products including internet banking and advanced mobile banking for your cell
phone and smartphones. In today's ever changing world we continue to grow and
change as necessary while keeping our rural, community based service a top
priority!

Our branches are staffed with employees who are part of your community. As
well, our board of directors is made up of local citizens, who guide us in
being relevant to you. We are here to provide you with local access to a
complete range of products and services from highly qualified professionals.
Not only do our people have the credentials, they also use 'local sense' to
deliver value and understanding. Radius Credit Union delivers the most
comprehensive line of products and services to our trading area. We are
committed to serving you for the long term!
